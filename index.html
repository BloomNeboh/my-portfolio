About

Once a kid sketching routes on the back of bus tickets in Arusha, I learned that hospitality is a practice of listening. I trained at the National College of Tourism, sharpened my front-desk craft at Airport Planet Lodge, and now I build experiences—one warm welcome at a time. My work blends precise process with a human beat: efficient, empathetic, and forward-thinking.

Design with purpose

I shape clear systems so guests feel guided, not guided through a script.

Service, not spectacle

Reliability is my headline; surprise and delight are the chorus.

Always learning

Languages: English, Swahili, learning French. Interests: traveling, hitchhiking & adventure tours.
